3rd Chakra · Personal power

Solar Plexus Chakra: when you lose courage, strength, and direction.

The Solar Plexus represents your need for action, self-esteem, achievement, and personal power. When it is balanced, you feel confident to act. When it is out of balance, you swing between freezing up, shrinking yourself, blowing up, or constantly trying to prove your worth.

Core needPower, confidence, and achievement
When balancedCourage, discipline, and leadership
When dysregulatedFear, anger, shame, or control

The Solar Plexus problem is not wanting to win, grow, or be recognized. The problem is when your strength gets blocked by fear, or distorted by ego. The Soul Game teaches you to turn personal power into conscious action.

Most common problems

Do you recognize yourself in any of these situations?

🪞

Low self-esteem

⏳

Procrastination

You know what you need to do, but you freeze when it is time to act, decide, or put yourself out there.

👁️

Fear of judgment

Other people's opinions weigh too heavily, and that dulls your spontaneity and initiative.

🚧

Trouble setting boundaries

You take on more than you should, avoid conflict, and later build up anger or resentment.

🔥

Anger and outbursts

Your action energy turns into irritation, impatience, harshness, or a need to win at any cost.

🏆

Need for approval

Your worth becomes dependent on results, status, recognition, or outside validation.

The need underneath

What do all of these problems have in common?

They are all different ways of dealing with the same need: to feel that you have strength, worth, and the ability to act in the world.

When that need is blocked, you shrink. When it is in excess, you try to dominate. The healthy path is to develop personal power with awareness, responsibility, and direction.

The positive and negative form

Every need can be met in two ways.

A balanced Solar Plexus does not erase ambition, strength, or the desire to grow. It turns that energy into proportionate action, responsible leadership, and achievement with awareness.

Healthy form

  • Acting despite fear, in proportionate steps
  • Building real skills and competence
  • Setting boundaries with clarity and respect
  • Leading without dominating
  • Pursuing growth without depending on approval

Negative form

  • Avoiding action so you never fail or get judged
  • Dominating, competing, or proving your worth at any cost
  • Confusing strength with harshness
  • Swallowing your limits until you explode
  • Measuring self-worth by results alone

Starter protocol

What to do when the Solar Plexus pattern kicks in?

1

Name the block

Ask yourself: "Am I afraid of failing, of being judged, of displeasing someone, or of losing control?" Naming it separates you from the pattern.

2

Choose a proportionate action

Do not try to fix your whole life. Choose one small action that proves movement: send it, ask, decide, say no, begin.

3

Build power through evidence

After you act, log it: "I did it." The Solar Plexus grows stronger when it stacks up real proof of capability, not just positive thoughts.
