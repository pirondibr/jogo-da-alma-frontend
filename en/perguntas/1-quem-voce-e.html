Question 01 of 7

Who are you?

Have you ever compared yourself to someone and concluded that the problem was you? Have you tried to be more disciplined, more outgoing, more daring, and felt like you were rowing against your own nature? Maybe the problem was never effort. Maybe it was the ruler you were measuring by.

The Game's answer

You are not one single thing. You are a unique blend of seven frequencies, and the way they combine in you shapes your personality, defines your talents, your blind spots, where you find happiness, and where you produce results.

Wide · The scale of the universe

You are bigger than you think

Before any test, type, or label, there is a more fundamental fact: you are not merely in the universe, you are the universe experiencing itself. And experience is only possible through duality. You only know courage in the face of fear, connection in the face of loneliness, safety in the face of risk. Every opposition you live through is not a flaw in existence. It is how existence works.

But this experience does not happen in some diffuse way. It happens through seven channels.

Medium · The mechanics of identity

Seven frequencies. One blend that is yours alone.

The seven frequencies of human experience, Safety, Pleasure, Power, Connection, Expression, Insight, and Meaning, exist in everyone, at some level. Nobody has zero of anything. The board is the same for everyone: what changes is the intensity of each frequency in you. Here is the map of a real person:

All seven are present, but usually 1 to 3 sit at the high end. Those are the dominant ones: in the example above, a Spiritual-Intellectual-Empathetic person. Your configuration is different, and that is exactly what the quiz reveals.

3×

Your dominant frequencies amplify everything they stand for by three times: the needs, the talents and the shadows. You did not choose to be this way. This is your natural frequency. And the point was never to change who you are: it is to align your life with who you already are.

Personality is pleasure, not lack

Your personality is made of the frequencies you most enjoy and take pleasure in living: where talent flows, where time flies, where you recharge. It is your nature.

But it is not necessarily what you lack most in your life. In the example above, the person takes pleasure in meaning, in ideas, and in connections, while the game may be demanding exactly the low frequencies: safety, energy, action. Who you are is one thing. What your life is asking for right now is another.

That is why the Game measures the two separately: the personality quiz reveals where your pleasure lives; the diagnosis reveals where your deficiency lives.

Each full profile reveals: your strengths and shadows in detail, the environment where you thrive (and where you wither), the right ruler for holding yourself accountable, your formula for success, the dynamics with the other profiles, and the recalibration questions.

You are probably not just one profile

If dominance runs from one to three frequencies, pure profiles are the minority. Most people are a blend, and the blend changes the whole game:

Ambitious + Intellectual

The strategist. Grows by mastering systems: studies the game before playing it, and wins on depth, not on force.

Ambitious + Empathetic

The people leader. The same hunger for growth, but the trophy is the whole team rising together. Suffers when forced to choose between results and relationships.

Traditional + Spiritual

Root and sky: the two opposite frequencies of the map in the same person. An internal engine of tension: the ground asks for safety, the heights ask for meaning. When integrated, they turn into rare depth.

That is why The Soul Game does not hand you a box, it hands you your configuration: which frequencies dominate, in what proportion, and how they talk to each other.

The 3× effect in practice

Dominance is not a personality detail: it changes the physics of your life. The same person lives two completely different worlds depending on where they play:

  • You learn 3× faster

  • Relationships flourish
  • Life shifts into constant growth mode

When you play outside it

  • Your capacity plummets
  • You are slow to learn

  • Unhappiness becomes routine

It is not a lack of effort. It is not laziness. It is frequency misalignment, and the way out starts with knowing, precisely, what yours is.

Echoes in other traditions

The idea that there are human types, each with its own gifts and traps, runs through history: from the temperaments of Greek medicine to the Enneagram, from the archetypes of psychology to modern typologies. They are echoes of the same observation. In The Soul Game, though, the structure is its own: the profile is not a box you are born trapped in: it is the dominance of frequencies. And frequencies have states, they develop, and they evolve from one level to the next.
