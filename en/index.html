Question 01

Who you are?

You are not just the universe experiencing itself through duality: you are also a being made of 7 different frequencies that shape your personality, your way of thinking, feeling and acting.

Your personality has a huge influence on your strengths, your weaknesses, where you will be happy and where you will produce the most. Your relationships, the way you make money, what you enjoy, your style of motivation and discipline: all of it changes drastically depending on your personality.

The Game's answer

  • 7 personality archetypes: Traditional / Conservative, Creative / Dynamic, Ambitious, Empathetic, Expressive, Intellectual, Spiritual
  • Every profile has strengths nobody ever taught you to value
  • And predictable shadows, which stop running you once they have a name

Question 02

Why the world is the way it is?

The world looks chaotic, cruel and pointless, but there is an explanation. Our brain was shaped over hundreds of thousands of years in a hostile environment, and it keeps running that old software in a world that has already changed.

Fear, scarcity, tribalism and domination are not your essence. They are Human 1.0. And a society built by Human 1.0 becomes a Civilization 1.0.

The old software in action

  • Societies organized by fear
  • A culture of addiction and consumption
  • Predatory competition and obsession with status
  • Polarization, propaganda and dogmas

Question 03

Why we are here?

Life works like a game. Not a trivial game: a system of experiences with rules, challenges, learning and growth.

We are here to grow. And in The Soul Game, the negative is the gateway to the positive: every challenge that shows up in your life is pointing exactly to the frequency asking for your next move.

Every game has three elements

  • An objective: to grow from 1.0 to 2.0
  • A scoring system: not one, but seven
  • Mechanics: the rules for how to score in a positive way

Question 04

How to reach happiness?

Your happiness is proportional to your ability to score across the seven frequencies of human experience, the energy centers observed for thousands of years.

The key to the game: to fill each need in a way that is positive in the short and long term, for you and for the people around you. The same need can be filled by a path that builds or by one that charges interest.

Question 05

How to reach material success?

Material success is not luck, and it is not a frequency of its own. It is the result of playing specific frequencies well: the security you build, the skills you develop, the value you manage to communicate.

And each one has a positive form and a negative form. Hoarding out of fear is not the same as building stability. Dominating people is not the same as leading.

The positive path

  • Root: build financial stability and plan for the future
  • Solar Plexus: develop skills, overcome challenges, build something meaningful
  • Throat: communicate ideas and make your value heard
  • Third Eye: understand the systems of the game before you play

Question 06

What is our future?

The future is not decided: it is the sum of how each player plays. Over the last two centuries, humanity has already proven it can update itself: life expectancy doubled, child mortality plummeted, extreme poverty shrank.

The next step is not technological. It is Civilization 2.0: the same update you make on the inside, done at scale.

Civilization 1.0 → 2.0

  • From fear → to structures that guarantee safety
  • From addiction → to conscious and creative pleasure
  • From predatory competition → to responsible leadership
  • From polarization → to cooperation across differences
  • From propaganda → to transparent communication
  • From dogmas → to free spirituality

Question 07

What is your part in that future?

You don't update the world by decree: you update it by playing your own game better. Every frequency you learn to fill in a positive way changes you, and changes the environment around you.

There are three challenges, in this order: know the game, update yourself, and take part in the collective update. The seventh question is only answered by living the other six.

The 3 pillars

  • Knowledge: understand the 7 frequencies and how to fill them
  • Personal update: transcend the patterns of fear, scarcity and domination
  • Collective update: be part of the shift from a Civilization 1.0 to a 2.0
